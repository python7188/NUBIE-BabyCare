Our journey started with a simple belief.

Baby care products should be safe, gentle, and thoughtfully designed. When we couldn't find skincare that met our uncompromising standards for purity without sacrificing elegance, we partnered with leading pediatricians to create our own.

Every bottle, every balm, and every bundle is a promise from our family to yours: unconditional care for the moments that matter most.

We believe transparency is the ultimate luxury. Our philosophy rejects the notion that effective care must rely on harsh synthetics. Instead, we embrace a minimalist approach: using only what is necessary, and ensuring every single ingredient serves a calming purpose.

Skin-Safe Materials

We source ultra-pure colloidal oatmeal, refined shea, and calendula to actively support the developing lipid barrier.

Pediatrician Approved

Every formula undergoes strict dermatological reviews to guarantee tear-free, rash-relieving results.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Our soft-touch bottles are designed to look beautiful in your nursery and break down responsibly in the earth.

Behind the Brand

"We wanted peace of mind, bottled."

As new parents, the sheer volume of choices was overwhelming. The market was flooded with 'natural' products that still harbored hidden irritants, and effective products that felt clinical and harsh.

Nubie was born from our desire to bridge that gap. We envisioned a brand that felt like a deep breath—a luxurious, fail-proof toolkit for parents who refuse to compromise on aesthetics or safety.

Sarah & Michael Thompson Founders, Nubie
